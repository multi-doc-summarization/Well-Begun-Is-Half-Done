a pakistani court has ruled that an american diplomat who allegedly shot and killed two men he says tried to rob him must remain in custody for 14 more days , cnn reports . raymond davis ’ lawyer quickly filed a petition for his release , holding that he has diplomatic immunity . davis has been transferred from police custody to “ judicial remand ” —which usually means prison . a hearing is scheduled for feb. 25 . the us has called for his release , and american officials have threatened to cut aid to pakistan over the matter . meanwhile , a pakistani police chief called davis ’ actions “ clear-cut murder. ” he said witnesses reported that davis continued firing at the men as one ran away . he admits that the men were armed , and that one pointed a gun at davis , but says they didn ’ t fire .
