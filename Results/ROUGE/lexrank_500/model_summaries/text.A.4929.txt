" it 's about allowing our fans who have been looking and trying to get a hold of our music to have access to it , " says one of the trio , posdnuos . that has n't always been easy because the band 's heavy use of samples has led to copyright headaches , explains rolling stone . slate , meanwhile , picks out 10 de la soul songs perfect for valentine 's day .
