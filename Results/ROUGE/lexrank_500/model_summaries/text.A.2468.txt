a man and two children are dead in india after a kite flying tradition on independence day went horribly wrong . two children , aged 3 and 4 , had their throats slit by kite strings covered with powdered glass or metal—a coating used to cut down competitors ' kites—while riding with their heads out of car sunroofs in separate incidents in delhi on monday , reports the bbc . a 22-year-old motorcyclist suffered a fatal fall when he became entangled in a string , per the indian express , while a police officer was also injured . the delhi government has since banned the sale of the sharp strings .
