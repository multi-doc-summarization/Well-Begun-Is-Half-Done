afghan police today fired into a crowd of rock-hurling anti-american demonstrators chanting " death to america , " " death to christians , " and " death to karzai " in kabul today . one was killed and at least five injured , two from gunshots . the us koran-burning controversy has stirred days of outrage and protests among millions of muslims and others worldwide , notes the ap ; today 's protests were the largest so far , reuters adds . afghan police say the taliban are using this anger to fuel anti-government sentiment ahead of elections next week . " there are more than 10,000 of the demonstrators and some of them are waving the taliban flag , " says one officer .
