beyonce will not be adding " basketball team owner " to her impressive resume , at least not yet . the singer was rumored to be part of an investment group looking to buy the houston rockets , but the group lost out to billionaire restaurateur tilman fertitta , tmz reports . fertitta will reportedly buy the team from current owner leslie alexander for $ 2.2 billion ( a record price for an nba team , espn reports ) ; alexander originally bought the team for $ 85 million in 1993 . fertitta , who stars in the cnbc reality series billion dollar buyer , is reportedly worth $ 3.1 billion .
