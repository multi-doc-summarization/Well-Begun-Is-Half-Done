a pakistani court has sentenced a father , brother , and two cousins to death after they used stones and bricks to murder a pregnant relative who married without the family 's consent . the decision came yesterday , months after farzana parveen 's " honor killing " outside a lahore courthouse in may drew international attention , the new york times reports . another relative was handed 10 years in prison . the bbc reports the death sentences are somewhat unusual , and some suspect the sentences will be reduced on appeal . a lawyer for the men says they will indeed appeal the " politically motivated " sentences . prosecutors , however , say the men 's guilt was proven using dna evidence .
