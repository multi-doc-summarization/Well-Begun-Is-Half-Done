a mummified monk found in the lotus position in mongolia last week has been found on january 27 in the songinokhairkhan province , reports the siberian times . the monk was in a rare state of meditation called " tukdam " . " if the meditator can continue to stay in this meditative state , he can become a buddha , " says a professor at the mongolian institute of buddhist art at ulaanbaatar buddhist university , tells the mongolian times . " the mummified body sits in a lotus position , as if still meditating , " he said .
