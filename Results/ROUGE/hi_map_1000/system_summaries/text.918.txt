mcdonald 's has joined the fight against plastic pollution by switching to paper straws in all its uk and ireland restaurants , starting from september . it is the latest company to opt out of some single-use plastic products which can take hundreds of years to decompose if not recycled , the ap reports . " reflecting the broader public debate , our customers told us they wanted to see a move on straws , " the firm said , per the ap . " we hope this work will support industry wide change and bring sustainable solutions to scale , " he said .
