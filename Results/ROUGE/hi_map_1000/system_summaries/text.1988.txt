a hydroelectric dam collapsed in southeastern laos , leaving an unknown number of people dead and hundreds missing , state media said tuesday . rescue efforts were underway as top government officials rushed to the site and public appeals were launched for aid . the official lao news agency kpl said the firm was co-operating by a joint venture led by south korean companies , with thai and lao partners . the project was still under construction , kpl reported . it described the portion that collapsed as a " saddle dam , " which is an auxiliary dam used to hold water beyond what is held by the main dam . a spokesman for the one of the companies involved in the project said heavy rain and flooding caused the collapse . " we do not have any formal information yet about any casualties or how many are missing , " an attapeu official tells the afp .
