" finally , the tiger could n't hold back , grabbed the goat by the withers and tossed him , " park director dmitry mezentsev tells state news agency tass on friday . " we decided to put them in different enclosures for a while , " he said , per the new york times . " time will tell if we will return him to amur , it ’ s too early to tell . for the moment , he needs to recover and to rest. ” mezentsev was then taken to the vet who examined him and said he was in good health , but needed rest after the shock .
