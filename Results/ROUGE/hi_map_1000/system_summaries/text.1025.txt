the italian captain of the costa concordia ship , francesco schettino , has returned to the wreck for the first time as part of his manslaughter trial . the ship hit a reef near the island of giglio in january 2012 and capsized , with the loss of 32 lives . he denies the charges of manslaughter and abandoning ship , which could see him jailed for up to 20 years . he arrived on the island off the tuscan coast on tuesday and is said to have wept when he saw the wreck from a ferry taking him to giglio . " they want to show that i am weak , just like two years ago . it 's not true , is making me nervous , " he said , per the afp . " there 's no real anger here any more . two years have passed and it 's time to make peace with what happened . " the bbc notes that the captain 's former employer , costa crociere , had shifted the whole world on his own . " it is very , very difficult because schettino does not have the economic resources of costa , " schettino tells afp .
