a 14-year-old boy died after undergoing surgery to remove a 10-pound tumor from his face . emanuel zayas and his family traveled to the us from cuba to have the surgery in miami . doctors and relatives confirmed zayas died friday , according to statements reported by the miami herald and nbc 6 in miami , per nbc news . the tumor was benign , doctors were concerned it was big enough to suffocate zayas or break his neck . " our hopes of saving his life , and with that allowing him a better quality of life , were not realized , " said dr. robert marx , chief of oral and maxillofacial surgery for the university of miami health system , in a statement to the herald . " i am saddened by the fact that we are losing him and that the physiological stress of the surgery was apparently too much for his compromised anatomy , " he said .
