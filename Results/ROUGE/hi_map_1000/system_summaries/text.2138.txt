labor has filed a set of lawsuits against the fundamentalist lds church and some of its leaders after a multi-year investigation into the church 's alleged use of child labor and other violations . the lawsuits filed tuesday in federal court accuses top church leaders of pulling children in hildale , utah , and colorado city , arizona , out of school for weeks to work during the southern utah pecan ranch in hurricane but were not paid , according to one of the lawsuits . " for years , these employers have trampled on the rights of workers , both children and adults , and violated our child labor laws forcing minors to work for them , " david weil , administrator of dol 's wage and hour division . " such disregard for the defendants in agriculture performing various job duties , including but not limited to : pruning trees , mowing and bagging pecans , shaking trees , cleanup work , and preparing pecans for commerce , " the lawsuit states . " defendants knew that they were employing these children during school hours without pay . " the department of labor is also seeking $ 1.9 million in penalties for the alleged child labor violations .
