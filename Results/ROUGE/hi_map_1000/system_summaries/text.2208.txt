rudy giuliani will not serve in donald trump 's incoming administration , the ap reports . " rudy giuliani is an extraordinarily talented and patriotic american . i will always be appreciative of his 24/7 dedication to our campaign after i won the primaries and for his extremely wise counsel , " he said . " he is and continues to be a close personal friend , and as appropriate , i will call upon him for advice and can see an important place for him in the administration at a later date . " giuliani , 72 , pulled out of the secretary of state sweepstakes late last month , a description contradicted by cnn reporting that giuliani removed himself from consideration for a position last month . " the whole thing was becoming kind of very confusing and very difficult for the president-elect , and my desire to be in the cabinet was great , but it was n't that great , " giuliani says . " you can make friends and make up , but ... i would not see him as the candidate for the cabinet . " trump 's transition team aide named romney , tillerson and corker as the front-runners for the job , adding that giuliani 's self-promotion had hurt his case .
