pope francis on thursday exhorted the united states to summon a " spirit of cooperation " to help the death penalty a thing of the past . " every life is sacred , every human person is endowed with an inalienable dignity , and society can only benefit from the rehabilitation of those convicted of crimes , " he said . " now is the time for courageous actions and strategies , aimed at implementing a culture of care and an integrated approach to combating poverty , restoring dignity to the excluded , and at the same time protecting nature , " the pope said in his prepared speech to a joint meeting of congress . the speech was the first time a pope had addressed congress . " i am convinced that we can make a difference and i have no doubt that the united states—and this congress—have an important role to play , " said the pope pivoted . " not only do i support them , but i also offer encouragement to all those who are convinced that a just and necessary punishment must never exclude the dimension of hope and the goal of rehabilitation. . "
