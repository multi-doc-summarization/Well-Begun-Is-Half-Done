two skydivers were killed and one was injured following a mid-air collision late tuesday afternoon in eloy , officials say . brian jerome said the accident happened around 200 feet from the ground and caused the skydivers 's parachutes to collapse as they fell , reports the ap . a third skydiver was dead when authorities arrived on scene and a second died at a local hospital , the ap reports . the group had been part of a 200-member group participating in a week-long event at skydive arizona , which operates near eloy municipal airport . officials say the skydivers had been working to create “ as many new ‘ official ’ world sequential records as we can ” by friday , az reports . jerome says the deceased were not american , but he declined to name their countries of origin . investigators are still trying to determine how the collision occurred and plan to interview witnesses throughout the night , jerome added . the cause of the collision is under investigation . police have not yet released names of any of the people involved . the collision are not believed to be serious . the injuries occurred at the scene and another died in a nearby hospital .
