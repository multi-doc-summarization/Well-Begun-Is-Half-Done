authorities say a man who held up a denver bank got a cab ride to and from the robbery . the fbi wants the public 's help to find the suspect . authorities say the man took a cab to the bank , asked the driver to idle while he went inside , and then had the cabbie drive him to denver international airport . officials say it 's unknown if the suspect actually boarded a plane after going to the airport . video from inside the cab clearly shows the man 's face , and pictures taken in the public service credit union shows him wearing what looks like a white dust mask over his lower face . he wore a black beanie cap , dark gray jacket , black shirt with white letters , dark jeans and dark sunglasses . anyone with information can call metro denver crime stoppers at 720-913-7867 , or text 274637 with the title dmcs , and a message . he got into the cab near the bellco theater in downtown denver and took it to the credit union at 6850 s. yosemite st. , where he threatened a small pistol and ordered a teller to give him an undisclosed amount of money before fleeing the bank .
