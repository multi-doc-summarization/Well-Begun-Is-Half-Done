first ww , now another high-profile name change . dunkin ' donuts will as of january be known simply as dunkin ' , in what nbc news reports is a rebranding designed to reflect its broadened focus on coffee and other beverages and up its appeal to a younger audience . cnn describes it as wanting to be seen as a " beverage-led " company . the logo may be down a word , but the company says it wo n't otherwise look different : it will still use orange and pink and the rounded font it has had in use since 1973 , with the pink part now becoming the apostrophe . and yes , stores will still serve doughnuts .
