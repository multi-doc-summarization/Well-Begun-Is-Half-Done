at least one person was killed and multiple others injured friday by a gunman inside new york city 's bronx lebanon hospital , the ap reports . the shooter , a doctor who used to work at the hospital , also apparently killed himself . a law enforcement official anonymously named dr. henry bello as the suspect ; it 's not clear when he left the hospital . the ap says six others were injured while cnn puts the number at five , three of them with serious injuries . per cnn , the victim who was killed is a woman . the new york times says three of the people shot are doctors .
