wikileaks ' founder has a " thermonuclear device " of unreleased files that he says he 'll detonate if he 's killed or brought to trial . julian assange has widely distributed an encrypted 1.3-gigabyte file containing the full versions of all the us documents the site has received , and his lawyer says the encryption key will be released if assange is forced to appear before swedish authorities on sexual assault charges or american authorities on treason charges , the globe and mail reports . assange has referred to the file as his insurance policy , claiming it contains names , addresses , and other details that were removed to protect spies , soldiers , and other sources . wikileaks " has been subject to cyber attacks and censorship around the world and they need to protect themselves . this is what they believe to be a thermonuclear device in the information age , " said assange 's lawyer .
