it looks like a good day for jay-z : sprint is buying a 33 % stake in his music-streaming service , tidal . billboard reports the deal was for $ 200 million . under the terms , jay-z and other big-name celebs—including beyonce , madonna , and kanye west—will remain part-owners . sprint , meanwhile , will be able to provide exclusive music to its customers . the deal may be a life-saver for tidal , which has been struggling to compete with apple music , pandora , and spotify , reports the new york times . its holding company lost about $ 28 million in 2015 . ( jay-z maintains that having artists own the site is what sets tidal apart . )
