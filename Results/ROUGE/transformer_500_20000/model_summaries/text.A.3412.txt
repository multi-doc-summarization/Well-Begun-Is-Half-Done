two bodyguards who worked for tom brady and gisele bundchen were sentenced to five years in prison for attempted murder over an incident during the couple 's april 2009 wedding , sky news reports . miguel solis and alexander rivas demanded two photographers in costa rica give up their camera memory cards ; as the photographers drove off , at least one of the bodyguards shot at them . solis and rivas were also ordered to pay $ 10,000 to each photographer . neither was hurt in the incident , of which bundchen claimed to be unaware back in 2009 .
