the nebraska cornhuskers lost punter sam foltz in a car accident in july , and the football team paid him tribute in its first game of the season on saturday : after their first drive ended with a fourth and four , the team lined up solemnly in punt formation with just 10 men and stood without a punter for a minute as the crowd cheered , reports sports illustrated . the cornhuskers took a delay of game penalty for their tribute , notes deadspin . foltz was the big ten punter of the year last season .
