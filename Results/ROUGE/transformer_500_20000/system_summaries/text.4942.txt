there 's no evidence of foul play or violence in celebrity chef anthony bourdain 's death in a french hotel room , a french prosecutor tells the ap . " there is no element that makes us suspect that someone came into the room at any moment , " he says . " it is with great respect for the leader , the tv entertainer , the visionary anthony bourdain that i express all my condolences to his family and to the anonymous people around the world who he made dream so much , " says olivier nasti , the chef and owner of le chambard , the luxury hotel in kaysersberg where bourdain took his life , paid tribute to his colleague saturday . nasti said there did not appear to be much planning in the television personality 's suicide , including urine tests , to see if the 61-year-old american took any medications or other drugs , in an effort to help his family understand if anything led him to kill himself . nasti said toxicology tests were being carried on bourdain 's body in the room . " we are not aware of any violence on bourdain , " nasti said in a statement . " it 's the whole family of french gastronomy that joins me , to renew our deep friendship to our bereaved american brothers . "
