a body found on a rural property in northwest spain is most likely that of an american woman who went missing while walking along a pilgrimage route , officials said saturday . interior minister jorge diaz said evidence pointed to the remains being those of denise thiem , 41 , and the 39-year-old prime suspect arrested over her disappearance was responsible for her death . a ministry spokesman had earlier said miguel angel munoz had led investigators to his small farm near the camino de santiago , after being arrested friday in asturias , 200 miles east of santa catalina de somoza , which is also on the st. james way and is the closest to munoz 's farm , reports the ap . the remains found at the property " effectively appear to those of mr. denise , " said diaz , who was last seen walking along the route at the town of astorga . thiem vanished after a police operation found him in the province of asturias , where he had fled . the embassy in madrid declined comment on the cause of death , but the ap reports that the suspect was described by the ministry as a man who " was little-known in his own neighborhood and did not relate to his disappearance . "
