a fire swept through a crowded nightclub in southern brazil early sunday , killing at least 245 people and leaving at least 200 injured , police say . a man carries an injured victim of a fire at the kiss club in santa maria city , the ap reports . " smoke filled the place instantly , the heat became unbearable , " a medical student tells globonews . " people could not find the only exit . they went to the toilet thinking it was the exit and many died there . " fire officials said at least one exit was locked and that club , who at first thought those fleeing were trying to skip out on bar tabs , initially blocked patrons from leaving . the cause of the fire is not yet known , officials say . the death toll would make it one of the deadliest nightclub fires more than a decade . the fire brigade 's owner says it was at its maximum capacity of between 1,000 and 2,000 , and the 245 partygoers were pushing and shoving to escape . ezekiel corte real , 23 , was crushed in the stampede .
