barry byron mills , the murderous leader of the aryan brotherhood during a stint at san quentin prison in the 1970s , was found dead july 8 in his single-person cell at the supermax prison in florence , colorado . he was 70 and serving multiple life sentences . a native of the san francisco bay area , according to the mercury news , was known for committing brazen , brutal killings , including a savage decapitation while in federal custody in georgia . mills , also known by the nickname " baron , " was known as baron , aggressively recruiting throughout the state . " i 'm not trying to glorify him , but i can tell you this : he had the admiration of a lot of inmates , but he was also feared , " retired federal prison warden robert hood tells the newspaper . mills , who became leader , says his grip on the gang stretched nationwide but was strongest in northern california , where many of its highest-ranking members came from . " there 's no doubt of his influence in the bay area . it 's fact , not debatable , " he says , adding that he has been given the death penalty for his crimes , but that he 's not sure he 'll be able to recover from his sentence . " he 's going to die , but it 's not going to be a good day , " hood says in a statement , per the ap , which notes that mills ' death has been ruled out by the state 's aryan brotherhood .
