dunkin ' donuts will drop the " donuts " from ads , packages and signage at new and remodeled stores , nbc news reports . doughnuts are still on the menu , but the company is renaming itself as a " beverage-led " to reflect its increasing emphasis on coffee and other drinks . " our new branding is one of many things we are doing as part of our blueprint for growth to modernize the dunkin ' experience for our customer , " dunkin ' brands ceo david hoffmann said in a statement . the company says it plans to open 1,000 new stores by the end of 2020 , and it will take place in january , nbc reports . dunkin ' also announced the change , though it 's also simplifying its menu and adding mobile ordering , which is expected to make up 60 % of the company 's us sales . the company has said that it was considering the change . the move comes after a new name will be adopted by international stores , the los angeles times reports . dunkin ' says the company will drop its donuts as well .
