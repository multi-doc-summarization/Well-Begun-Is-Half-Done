two men from the jersey shore are facing criminal charges after police say they stole a taxi to rob a house in town , nj.com reports . kenneth burke , 46 , and timothy foote , 38 , were charged with burglary , conspiracy to commit burglary , criminal trespass , and theft . according to the asbury park press , burke allegedly returned to the car with a tv set and refused to pay their fare . police say burke and foote were arrested on suspicion of burglary and theft . they were taken to monmouth county correctional institution and their bail was set at $ 20,000 . according to nj.com , the driver called police after he took the two to an apartment in asbury park , where they got out without paying the fare . the driver then took them to the apartment and returned a short time later . police say the two men were arrested in the incident . burke was arrested in a nearby jail on $ 20,000 bail . police say he has been burglarized , and he is expected to recover from the stolen goods from the homeowner 's home .
