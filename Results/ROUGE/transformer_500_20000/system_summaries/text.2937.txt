cardinal donald wuerl , the archbishop of washington , will travel to the vatican " in the very near future " to ask pope francis to accept his resignation , the pittsburgh post-gazette reports . in a letter to priests in the diocese , wuerl said he will meet with francis about the resignation of pope francis in rome . " our discernment here , i believe , has indicated the way forward to bring healing and a new beginning at the service of this church , " he said .
