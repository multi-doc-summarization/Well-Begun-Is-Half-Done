a zookeeper in japan has died after a white tiger mauled him at a zoological park . akira furusho , 40 , was sedated with a tranquiliser before rescue workers and police arrived , the bbc reports . he was taken to hospital and pronounced dead at the scene of a tiger attack in its enclosure at the hirakawa zoological park in kagoshima . the male tiger , named riku , was found bleeding from the neck in a tiger 's cage at the time of the attack , according to a zoo official . " we plan not to kill riku and continue to keep it because the bereaved family asked us to do so , " says a spokesperson . the zoo is investigating how the zoo operates and how it handles safety at work . the zookeeper , who visited the zoo in 1972 , is run by a public corporation under contract with the government . the tiger is about 1.8 inches in length and weighs about 170 pounds . the zoo 's manual forbids a keeper from entering the display cage , but it 's not clear whether the tiger was killed .
