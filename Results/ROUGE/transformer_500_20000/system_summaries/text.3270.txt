navy admiral scott stearney was found dead in his bahrain residence saturday in bahrain , reports nbc news . " this is devastating news for the stearney family , for the team at fifth fleet , and for the entire navy , " adm. john richardson said in a statement . " team , it 's my sad duty to inform you that today the secretary of the navy and i were informed that vice adm. scott stearney , our commander of u.s . naval forces central command and the combined maritime forces are cooperating on the investigation . " stearney was a decorated professional and a devoted father , and a good friend . he graduated from the university of notre dame in 1984 . he served as a bachelor of arts in national resource strategy from the national defense university . he served 36 years in the navy before joining forces in afghanistan in 2002 . he was found buried in his residence on saturday , and no foul play is suspected . the naval criminal investigative service and the ministry of naval operations says it has " no evidence of foul play at this time .
