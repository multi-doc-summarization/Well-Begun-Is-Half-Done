oscar pistorius has been sentenced to five years for culpable homicide in the death of his girlfriend reeva steenkamp , reuters reports . prosecutors will seek a murder conviction . pistorius will be released in august from a prison in south africa after serving 10 months of his five-year sentence under house arrest in august . according to the guardian , pistorius is eligible for release from prison to serve the remainder of his sentence . " our lives will never be the same again as live with the sadness of her death every day , " family member reeva steenkamp said in a statement . " we have forgiven mr pistorius even though he took the life of our precious daughter reeva . " pistorius ' family , we do not seek to avenge her death and we were all robbed of her life when she was killed . " pistorius , who was convicted of the lesser charge , has been incarcerated at the pretoria prison in august . he was released on parole by the end of august and is expected to be released aug. 21 . he will spend 15 years in prison if convicted .
