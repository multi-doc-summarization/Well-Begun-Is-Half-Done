a teenager fell from a stopped gondola at an upstate new york amusement park saturday , tumbling into a crowd of guests and employees below in an effort to catch the victim before she hit the ground . the warren county sheriff 's office said late saturday the 14-year-old girl was riding the attraction with a child relative , and fell about 25 feet from a two-person car , reports the ap . she was treated by emergency medical staff and transferred by ambulance to glen falls hospital before being taken by helicopter to the medical center . the ride was stopped by an operator after word was received that there was a rider in distress . " there does not appear to be any malfunction of the ride , but we have closed the attraction until a thorough review can be completed , " a park official says . the park said in a statement that the safety of guests is a top priority , the ap reports . the girl fell from the car and struck a tree before landing in the crowd . authorities say it was in proper working order . ( a scottish tourist was treated for back injury . )
