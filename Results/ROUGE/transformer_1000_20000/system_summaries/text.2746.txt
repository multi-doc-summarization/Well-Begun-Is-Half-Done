police arrested six students from a private all-boys catholic school in toronto monday and charged them with assault and sexual assault with a weapon , the ap reports . the high school is known for its sports programs and has produced many national hockey league players over the years . " the incidents of sexualized assault that happened to our students and on our school grounds are simply horrific , " toronto police inspector dominic sinopoli said in a statement . " we need to do much better at our culture and our student 's ability to talk to us . " sinopoli said the teens , who are all 14 and 15 , are from st. michael 's college and were captured on video . the school said it was conducting an independent examination into the " underlying attitudes and behaviors " of teens . the school 's principal did not attend a police press conference because of a bomb threat at the school , the school failed to notify police about the alleged assault . the video was posted on the internet , and police are investigating at least three other hazing incidents , including another alleged sexual assault . those arrested are young offenders and can not be named . they appeared in court monday and received bail .
