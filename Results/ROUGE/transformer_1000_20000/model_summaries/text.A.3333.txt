a chechen militant leader has claimed responsibility for the bombing that killed 36 people at russia 's biggest airport last month . warlord doku umarov appears in a 16-minute video posted online in which he says the suicide bombing at moscow 's domodedovo airport was carried out on his orders and warns of more attacks to come , ap reports . " you see this special operation carried out by my order . more special operations will be carried out in the future , " umarov says in the video . " among us there are hundreds of brothers who are prepared to sacrifice themselves " in further attacks . " we can at any time carry out operations where we want , " he adds . chechen rebels , who seek to establish an independent islamic state in the caucasus , also claimed responsibility for the moscow subway bombing last year .
