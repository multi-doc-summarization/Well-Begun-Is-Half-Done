twelve days after suicide bombs killed 16 people there , the brussels airport will reopen—at least partially—on sunday , reuters reports . brussels airlines will fly three flights—to greece , italy , and portugal—out of the airport that day . " a restart of the operations , even only partially , as quick as this is a sign of hope , " the airport 's chief executive says . according to bloomberg , new security measures will be in place for the reopening . vehicles approaching the airport will be randomly screened , travelers and baggage will be checked outside the airport , and there will be another security barrier inside the airport . trains and buses will not be servicing the airport for the time being , and cameras will read the license plates of vehicles arriving there . these measures will drastically reduce the amount of passengers the airport can handle , but officials hope to be back to normal capacity by july .
