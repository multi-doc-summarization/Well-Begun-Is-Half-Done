a little piece of history is on the market in california . an oceanfront estate in san clemente once owned by richard nixon and dubbed the western white house while he was in office is up for sale for $ 63.5 million , reports the los angeles times . nixon hosted everyone from leonid brezhnev to frank sinatra at the 5.45-acre estate , now owned by former pharmaceuticals exec gavin herbert . in addition to a 9,000-square-foot main house , the walled estate features a guesthouse , swimming pool , tennis court , greenhouse , 450 feet of beach front , and—given its location on an isolated bluff—panoramic ocean views . see a photo gallery here .
