about 10 people were sickened after a suspicious package arrived in the mailroom of an irs building in kansas city , missouri , friday morning , a fire official tells the ap . symptoms included vomiting and sweating ; two of the people were taken to a hospital but are in good condition . the package 's contents have not yet been determined , but the fire department is checking for gasses and fumes . the building was not evacuated , but the package has been isolated from the public . law enforcement is investigating , the kansas city star reports .
