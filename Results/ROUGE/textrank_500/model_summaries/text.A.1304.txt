toulouse gunman mohamed merah may be dead , but french authorities are eying complicity charges for his brother , abdelkader , reports sky news . " police inquiries have produced grave and matching pointers that suggest his ( abdelkader 's ) participation as accomplice in crimes relating to a terrorist enterprise is plausible , " says a statement from the paris prosecutor 's office . the elder merah , 29 , has been in custody since wednesday , and will be detained indefinitely as charges are weighed , notes the bbc . abdelkader merah 's companion , variously described as his wife or girlfriend , had also been held since wednesday , but was released earlier today .
