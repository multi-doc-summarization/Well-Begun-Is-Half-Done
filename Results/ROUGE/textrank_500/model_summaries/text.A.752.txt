the daughter of a man who died of lung cancer after smoking for 60 years has been awarded $ 80 million by a florida jury . the jury decided that tobacco giant rj reynolds was 90 % responsible for the man 's death at the age of 76 , and ordered the company to pay $ 8 million in compensatory damages and $ 72 million in punitive damages , businessweek reports . the woman 's lawyer expects the appeals process to last up to 3 years if the company appeals to florida 's supreme court , which he fully expects it to do . " tobacco always appeals , " he said . more than 7,000 smoking-related lawsuits are pending in florida , and the verdict against rj reynolds marks the end of an 8-case winning streak for the tobacco industry , the wall street journal notes .
