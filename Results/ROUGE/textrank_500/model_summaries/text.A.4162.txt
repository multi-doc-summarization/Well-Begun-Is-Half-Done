activating a turn signal takes just a flick of the finger , but that simple move apparently is too much for lots of drivers out there . a new patent filing by tesla seeks to change that . the company envisions an automated signal , one that senses when a driver is getting ready to turn and activates the blinker on its own , reports cnet . what electrek finds surprising about the patent application is that it appears to be designed for use with human drivers , not with self-driving cars . the technology stems from tesla 's autopilot function .
