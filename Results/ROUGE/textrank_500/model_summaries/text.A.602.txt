just days after retiring from 60 minutes , morley safer is dead at age 84 , reports cbs news . no word yet on a cause of death for safer , who had worked on the show about half a century . his retirement , however , was attributed to unspecified health issues , notes cnnmoney . prior to his work on 60 minutes , the canadian native made his name as a reporter covering the vietnam war for the network . as part of its going-away tribute surrounding safer 's final show sunday , cbs had posted videos celebrating his work here . " morley was one of the most important journalists in any medium , ever , " says cbs chief leslie moonves .
