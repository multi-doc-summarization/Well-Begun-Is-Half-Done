a fired nickelodeon producer facing allegations of sexual harassment is expressing regret over his behavior , the ap reports . chris savino , creator of the animated series the loud house , posted the apology on his facebook page . savino wrote monday that he 's " deeply sorry " and ashamed that his words and actions unintentionally created an uncomfortable environment . savino says he 's learned difficult but valuable lessons , adding that he respects the bravery of the women who have spoken out . savino has been accused of sexual harassment by up to 12 women , according to the website cartoon brew , which reports on animation industry news . last week , nickelodeon said it took allegations of misconduct seriously and that savino was no longer working with the children 's tv channel .
