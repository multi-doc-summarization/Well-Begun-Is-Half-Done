a man in india has been arrested for trying to sell his newborn grandson online . feroz khan , 47 , is alleged to have kidnapped the baby shortly after his birth in ludhiana , reports the afp , telling his daughter the infant was stillborn . he reportedly sold the baby for $ 830 to a nurse , who sold him to a hospital lab assistant for about $ 5,500 , who in turn sold him to a new delhi businessman for almost $ 15,000 , the hindu business line reports . police recovered the baby from the businessman 's home . five people have been arrested over the deals , and the baby was returned to his mother .
