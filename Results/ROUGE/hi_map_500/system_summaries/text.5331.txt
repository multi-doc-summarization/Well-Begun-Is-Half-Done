christoph mueller has confirmed that 6,000 jobs will be cut at the troubled airline - ahead of a rebrand in september which aims to cut about 6,000 jobs , the ap reports . the plan , released on monday by email , said the airline would begin operations under a new brand on september 1 . " the decline of performance started long before the tragic events of 2014 , " mueller says . " we are technically bankrupt , " says a news conference . the airline is operating as normal and no flights are currently affected . malaysia 's national carrier is also expected to pull back drastically from unprofitable long-haul routes such as to europe , and focus instead on regional traffic as it regroups . the announcement marks the first concrete move by newly-appointed ceo christoph mueller as he seeks to repair the damage caused by the losses of mh370 people on board in march 2014 , and just four months later , flight mh17 was downed by a suspected ground-to-air missile over ukraine - killing 298 people . the plane is still missing . in march last year , malaysia airlines will have a rebrand .
