there 's no evidence of foul play or violence in celebrity chef anthony bourdain 's death in a french hotel room , a french prosecutor said saturday . the famed cook , writer and host of the cnn series " parts unknown " killed himself friday in a luxury hotel in the ancient village of kaysersberg , christian de rocquigny , the prosecutor of colmar in france 's eastern alsace region , told the associated press in a phone interview . rocquigny said toxicology tests were being carried on bourdain 's body , including urine tests , to see if the 61-year-old american took any medications or other drugs , in an effort to help his family understand if anything led him to kill himself . olivier nasti , the chef and owner of le chambard , the visionary hotel in kaysersberg where bourdain took his life , paid tribute to his colleague saturday . " it is the whole family of french gastronomy that joins me , to renew our deep friendship to our bereaved american brothers , " he said , adding that there did not appear to be much planning in the television personality 's suicide .
