the israeli air force shot down a drone on saturday after palestinian militants fired rockets at southern israel , the ap reports . the israeli army says the incursion was a response to an israeli air strike that killed one militant and wounded a second a day earlier . israel said its air raid targeted 25-year-old mohammed makawi whom it linked to a radical group involved in a recent sinai border attack in march when nearly 300 rockets and the two sides fought a three-week war in december-january 2008-2009 . the drone was first spotted above the mediterranean near the hamas-ruled gaza strip to the west of israel , said military spokeswoman avital leibovich . the iron dome of hamas , the islamists who control the gaza strip , said it had targeted " hamas terror activity sites and terrorist squads responsible for the rocket fire " and gave no details . israeli forces say they will not remain passive in the face of what it called " one-sided " israeli violence . gaza has been under the control of hamas since 2007 . the border is tense , with frequent clashes . the armed army says over 470 rockets have been fired from gaza this year , but it was the first time since june that hamas had acknowledged launching rockets at israel .
