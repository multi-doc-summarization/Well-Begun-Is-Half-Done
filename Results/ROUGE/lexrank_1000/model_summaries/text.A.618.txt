a suicide bomber on an explosives-laden motorbike rammed the car of kandahar 's deputy governor today as he was on his way to work , killing him and wounding three of his bodyguards . the taliban have claimed responsibility , reports the ap , but the los angeles times notes that abdul latif ashna 's slaying raises the specter of last year 's wave of political assassinations around kandahar . the province is seen as vital in the afghanistan war . " the enemies of afghanistan can not stop the afghan people from development and progress by killing such personalities , " said president hamid karzai in a statement condemning the attack .
