jimmy carter was hospitalized in canada after he collapsed while building a habitat for humanity home thursday in winnipeg , cbc reports . the 92-year-old former president was using a handsaw to cut wood for a staircase at the time . the reason given for carter 's collapse was dehydration . according to the winnipeg free press , which has video of the incident , carter had been working in the sun for about 90 minutes . habitat for humanity states carter was taken to the hospital as a precaution and the former president is doing fine . " he encourages everyone to stay hydrated and keep building , " habitat for humanity ceo jonathan reckford says .
