as the sochi olympics kick off , google has taken a swipe at russia 's record on gay rights with its latest doodle . the doodle features images of winter athletes on the rainbow colors of the gay pride flag , along with a pointed quote from the olympic charter , business insider reports . " the practice of sport is a human right , " the quote reads . " every individual must have the possibility of practicing sport , without discrimination of any kind and in the olympic spirit , which requires mutual understanding with a spirit of friendship , solidarity , and fair play . " the doodle appears on google homepages in every country , including russia , mashable notes .
