a group of men in their 20s in washington state has posted a craigslist ad seeking a generic dad to grill burgers and hotdogs for a gathering set for the saturday of father 's day weekend in mid-june , the ap reports . qualifications include a minimum of 18 years of experience as a dad and 10 years of grilling experience . the successful dad must bring his own grill , but burgers and hotdogs will be provided . dane anderson tells khq-tv that the young men in spokane do n't live with their fathers and , the ad says , none are prepared to fill the role of barbecue dad . additional requirements include talking about dad things like lawnmowers , building your own deck , and musician jimmy buffett . payment is food and beer . anderson says several potential barbecue dads have responded .
